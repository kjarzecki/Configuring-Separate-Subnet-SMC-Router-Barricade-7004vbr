This script is linked to github repository. How to Configure a Separate Subnet on your Router

This webpage will outline how to configure a separate subnet on your router.
The end goal will be to disconnect your laptop from wifi, and route the internet connection through the router, on a subnet different from the router default gateway IP.
In this example we will be configuring an SMC Barricade 7004vbr.
